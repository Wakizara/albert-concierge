The Problem

Luxury travelers are lost.

Finding genuinely curated places in a new city is broken. Current solutions are either mass-market noise or outdated print guides.

  • Google Maps and TripAdvisor are crowdsourced - quality is unreliable
  • Instagram is superficial - beautiful photos, no real curation
  • Luxury print guides (LV, Monocle) are static and non-social
  • No platform connects premium travelers with similar tastes
  • The wealthy rely on word-of-mouth - inefficient and limited

Product

Key Features.

Smart 3-List System
"Want to Go" (bucket list), "Been There" (unlocks reviews), "Not Interested" (trains algorithm). Every swipe creates value.
Taste Profile
Auto-generated preference profile: "78% Fine Dining, 15% Rooftop Bars, 7% Concept Stores". Shareable on Instagram Stories for viral growth.
Interactive Map
MapBox dark theme with category filters, clustering, and offline access. Explore visually in any city.
ML Recommendation Engine
Every swipe trains the algorithm. Over time, Albert learns your taste better than you know it yourself.
Community Reviews
Only "Been There" users can rate and comment. No fake reviews. Authentic, premium feedback.
Admin-on-the-Go
Team members can add venues instantly from the field. Photo, GPS, and publish in one tap. Content scales with the team.

Strategy

Go-To-Market.

Phase 1: Concierge Clients
Convert existing high-net-worth concierge clients to app subscribers. Built-in trust, zero CAC. 50-100 subscribers day one.
Phase 2: Taste Profile Virality
Shareable Instagram Stories ("My taste on Albert") create organic growth. Users invite friends to compare profiles.
Phase 3: City Expansion
Each new city launch creates local buzz. Partner with luxury hotels, airlines, and concierge services for distribution.
Phase 4: Taste Match Network
As community grows, Taste Match connections become the primary retention driver. Social lock-in effect.
Phase 5: B2B Partnerships
Premium hotels, airlines (first class lounges), and credit card companies (Amex Centurion) as distribution channels.
  • Influencer seeding in luxury travel segment
  • Exclusive launch events in each city
  • PR in luxury publications (Monocle, Wallpaper*)
